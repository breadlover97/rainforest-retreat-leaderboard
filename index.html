❓ FAQ

Who can join this giveaway?

Everyone is welcome: existing clients, non-clients, and referrals.

How do I earn ballot chances?

You can earn ballot chances by completing a Financial Clarity Session, or when someone you refer completes theirs.

Can existing clients and non-clients earn ballots the same way?

Yes. The same rules apply to everyone.

Can I earn more than 1 ballot chance?

Yes. You can earn additional ballot chances through successful referrals.

When is a referral considered successful?

A referral is counted only after the referred person completes their Financial Clarity Session.

If I refer someone, do both of us get ballot chances?

Yes. Once they complete their session, they get 1 ballot for completing it, and you get referral ballot credit.

Can my referral refer someone else too?

Yes, but only after they complete their own Financial Clarity Session.

Who should fill in the form?

Only the person attending a Financial Clarity Session should fill in the form.

Do I need to fill in the form for someone I refer?

No. Ask them to fill in the form themselves and indicate your name as the person who referred them.

What happens if there are duplicate submissions?

The latest submission will be counted.
